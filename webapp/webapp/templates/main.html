About Project Influence Map

This project sets up to map the incoming and outgoing intellectual influence of academic entities -- people, projects, publications venues, and institutions. We do so by creating visualisations from the citation data curated in Microsoft Academic Graph.

Follow the links below to browse pre-curated visualisations from a selection of award winning academics or grow your own flowers containing any entity.

Methodology

Our main visual metaphor is the "influence flower", quantifying the bi-directional citations between the entity in question and the top entities it interacts with. We further allow filtering influence by time-frame. By default the person-centric citations filters out self-citations.
